## Detailed Explanation & Summary of GTM Concepts

Since this stage is less about the code and more about the "Market," here is a breakdown of what the judges are looking for with Avalanche-specific examples:

### 1. Product Vision vs. Roadmap

- **The Vision:** This is your "North Star." Judges want to know if you are building a one-off game or a platform.
- _Avalanche Example:_ "Our vision is to leverage Avalanche’s **Subnet** technology to provide a gas-free gaming experience for 1 million concurrent players by 2027."

- **The Roadmap:** This is the timeline of execution. For this competition, mention specific Avalanche milestones like moving from **Fuji Testnet** to **Mainnet** or deploying a **Custom L1 (Subnet)**.

### 2. Target User Personas

Don't just say "everyone." Define a specific person.

- **Example:** "The 'DeFi Gamer' — Someone who already uses **Core Wallet**, trades on **Trader Joe**, and wants to use their idle assets as in-game power-ups."

### 3. Player Acquisition Strategy (GTM)

In Web3, "Go-to-Market" often involves **Incentivized Growth**.

- **Avalanche Tip:** Mention the **Avalanche Foundation's** programs or partnering with existing Avalanche "Frontrunners" (early adopters/beta testers).
- **Growth Loop:** How does one user bring in another? (e.g., "Players earn a unique NFT skin for referring 3 friends who reach Level 5.")

### 4. Community & Guild Strategy

Guilds (like YGG) provide the "labor" for many Web3 games.

- **Explanation:** Explain how you will keep your Discord active. Will you have a **DAO**? Will you offer **Guild-specific leaderboards** to encourage competitive play?

### 5. Monetization Model

How does money flow?

- **Primary Sales:** Initial NFT mints (characters/land).
- **Secondary Fees:** Taking a **2.5% cut** of every trade on your marketplace.
- **Sinks:** Methods to remove tokens from circulation to prevent inflation (e.g., "Paying a small fee in $AVAX to upgrade a weapon").

### 6. Competitive Landscape

Be honest about who else is doing this.

- **The Edge:** Why is your game better on Avalanche than on Polygon or Solana?
- **Example:** "Unlike Game Z on Ethereum, our use of an Avalanche Subnet allows for instant finality and zero gas fees for players, making high-frequency gameplay possible."
